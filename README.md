# 🌱 GreenBatch AI

### Reducing Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Waste Through Predictive AI

---

## 📌 Overview

**GreenBatch AI** is an intelligent predictive platform designed to optimize pharmaceutical tablet manufacturing. It uses real-time process data and machine learning to **predict product quality before batch completion**, helping industries reduce waste, energy consumption, and production losses.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Pharmaceutical manufacturing faces significant challenges:

* Up to **15–20% of batches fail** due to process variability
* Quality issues are detected **only after production**
* Leads to:

  * Chemical waste
  * High energy consumption
  * Increased operational costs
  * Environmental impact

---

## 💡 Solution

GreenBatch AI introduces a **predictive manufacturing system** that:

* Monitors real-time parameters (temperature, pressure, moisture, etc.)
* Uses AI models to **forecast tablet quality**
* Detects risks **before batch completion**
* Suggests **real-time parameter adjustments**

👉 This transforms manufacturing from **reactive → preventive**

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. 📥 **Data Collection** – Collects real-time and historical manufacturing data
2. 📊 **Data Processing** – Cleans and analyzes process parameters
3. 🤖 **Predictive Modeling** – Predicts:

   * Tablet hardness
   * Dissolution rate
   * Quality deviation risk
     
4. 🚨 **Alert System** – Detects anomalies and risks
5. 🔧 **Optimization** – Suggests corrective actions
6. 📈 **Dashboard** – Displays insights for operators
